Interest in IT

My interest in started a couple years ago during my Biotech program, specifically in my genetics, bioinformatics, and microbiology courses. I quickly began to understand IT as the tool allowing us to push towards progress in these areas. The first human genome was sequenced in 1995. This took a total of 13 years to achieve. Today, we can do the same in under 2 days. This is due to innovation in IT, and the shortcuts it lent us in storing and analysing mass amounts of data. Genetic Technology and IT go hand-in-hand. Advancement in gene tech cannot happen without IT. They are both quickly growing industries and I want a hand in pushing both forward.
